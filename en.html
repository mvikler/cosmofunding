Frequently asked qustions about cosmofunding

GENERAL
What is cosmofunding?
cosmofunding is digitalizing brokerage for public and private company financing. The online platform automates the processes for private placements, creates transparency, shortens time-to-market and brings cost advantages.
GENERAL
Is there any obligation for a registered user to transact on the platform?
No, there is no obligation to perform transactions on the platform.
GENERAL
Who is behind cosmofunding?
cosmofunding belongs to Bank Vontobel AG and is therefore part of a Swiss bank established in 1924
GENERAL
What are the key benefits?
cosmofunding offers the fully automated securitization of private placements, which is unique in Switzerland, and provides an independent rating by fedafin.
Registration
How and at what cost can I register?
Issuers and Investors can gain free access through reaching out to cosmofunding directly via our registration form on the website.
Issuer
Is a rating mandatory for the credit request?
No, our exclusive partnership with fedafin, the independent and only Swiss rating agency, provides a rating.
Issuer
Is there a minimum amount for an issue?
The minimum issue volume is CHF 500'000.
Issuer
What forms of financing are available?
Private placements and loans
